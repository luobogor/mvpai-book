## About This Book
This book is an introductory guide to [MVPAI](https://mvpai.dev) technology. By following this book, you will learn how to build your own MVPAI.

[MVPAI](https://mvpai.dev) is an AI-based Next.js SaaS rapid development template that helps developers launch SaaS projects in minutes. With pre-built components and AI-assisted features, MVPAI accelerates the development process, allowing users to focus on core business logic. It supports modern technology stacks, including authentication, payment integration, and multilingual support, helping users quickly build high-quality MVPs.

### Why Write This Booklet?
1. To help users quickly familiarize themselves with the template's technology stack.
2. To document issues encountered during development, accumulate knowledge, and facilitate review.
3. To provide support for product promotion.
